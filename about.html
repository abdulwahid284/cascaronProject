Cascaron

The Cascaron a Crossed platform multi-collection modular mini-projects, provides high level tool sets to manage low level technologies.


The Cascaron is at it’s early stage, begins as a research project in IPRF@XLVM - International Private Research Foundataion, that aims to provide scalabilities and yet in C++14/17.

The involvement of Cascaron is in different areas like: Zero-copy data streaming, APIs commonised among different manufacturers. Cascaron supports both architecture 32 bits and 64 bits operating systems: x86_64/32, arm_64/32.
